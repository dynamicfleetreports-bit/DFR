1. Purpose

This agreement governs the referral partnership between Pieter Nell t/a Dynamic Fleet Reporting ("DFR") and the Referral Partner named above. The Partner refers potential clients to DFR in exchange for commission payments as defined herein. The Partner's role is strictly limited to referral and advertising — all client management, auditing, reporting, and service delivery remains exclusively DFR's responsibility.

2. The Partner's Role

The Partner shall promote DFR's fleet reporting and forensic auditing services to transport companies and fleet operators using their unique referral link. The Partner shall NOT make pricing commitments on DFR's behalf, contact or manage referred clients directly, represent themselves as a DFR employee, or solicit clients to competing services during or for 12 months after this agreement.

3. Commission Structure
💰 Commission Summary

Base rate: 15% of monthly retainer · Payable after client invoice confirmed paid · Setup fees excluded · Paid within 7 business days

  1. Rate: 15% of the client's monthly retainer (admin fee + vehicle rate). The once-off R2,500 setup fee is excluded.
  2. Trigger: Commission is only due after DFR has confirmed receipt of the client's payment.
  3. Duration: Commission is payable for the full lifetime of the client's contract, provided this agreement remains active.
  4. Payment: EFT to the Partner's nominated bank account within 7 business days of the monthly commission calculation date.
