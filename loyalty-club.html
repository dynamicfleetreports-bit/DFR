📋 DFR Loyalty Club — Reward Tiers

Clients earn rewards every month they refer new clients who activate. Credit is based on the referred client's fleet size.

Referrals that month
1
10% of new client's first month retainer as credit
e.g. 20-vehicle client = R500 credit
Referrals that month
2
15% of each (1.5× multiplier) as credit
e.g. 2 × 20-vehicle = R1,500 credit
Referrals that month
3–4
20% credit (2×) + 50% off next invoice
e.g. 3 × 20-vehicle = R3,000 + 50% off
Referrals that month
5+
30% credit (3×) + 50% off next invoice
e.g. 5 × 20-vehicle = R7,500 + 50% off
Annual Milestone — Every 10 Activated Referrals
Every time a client's annual counter reaches a multiple of 10, they earn 1 free month added to their bank. Free months never expire. Maximum 1 per invoice cycle. Clients choose when to use them — including saving for December.
