Referral Partner Programme — Key Terms

Commission: 15% of the monthly retainer (excluding setup fees) for each referred client, payable after the client's invoice is confirmed paid by DFR. Commission is paid within 7 business days of the monthly calculation date.

Your Role: You refer potential clients to DFR using your unique referral link. DFR handles all client interaction, auditing, reporting, and invoicing. You do not contact or manage clients on DFR's behalf.

Annual Bonus: Portfolio growth bonuses are assessed each November. Growth of 1–10% earns 1 extra month's commission; 11–20% earns 2 months; 21%+ earns 3 months.

Activation: Your application will be reviewed by DFR within 48 hours. You will receive a WhatsApp confirmation with your referral link once approved.

Confidentiality: All client information, pricing, and business processes are strictly confidential. You may not share client details with any third party.

Termination: Either party may end the partnership with 30 days notice. Commission on existing clients continues for 3 months post-termination.

Independent Contractor: You are not an employee of DFR. No UIF, PAYE, or employment benefits apply.
