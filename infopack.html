Who We Are

About Dynamic Fleet Reporting

Dynamic Fleet Reporting is a specialist fleet intelligence and auditing consultancy based in Moorreesburg, Western Cape. We partner with businesses that operate vehicle fleets — helping them understand exactly where fuel is being lost, how drivers are performing, and what it's costing them every month.

We provide data-driven, independently produced fleet performance reports — delivered electronically, on a regular cycle. Our work is built on a single principle: you can only improve what you can measure. We give fleet managers and business owners the numbers they need to make confident decisions.

What We Do

Our Services

Every subscription includes a core weekly/monthly fleet intelligence report, a pool of on-demand reports, and full account support. Here's exactly what you get.

Weekly / Monthly Fleet Intelligence Report

Included

Your core deliverable — a comprehensive report covering your entire active fleet. Delivered electronically within 24–48 hours of receiving all required data inputs.

  • Total fleet distance travelled (GPS-recorded & odometer-reconciled)
  • Total fuel consumed across all active vehicles
  • Fleet-wide actual vs benchmark fuel consumption
  • Estimated total fuel loss in litres and Rand (wholesale & retail price)
  • Per-vehicle efficiency analysis vs manufacturer-set target
  • GPS vs odometer reconciliation — drift flagged where applicable
  • Speed violation events, max speed, and over-speed duration per vehicle
  • Estimated financial impact of speed violations
  • Consumption anomaly flags (informational — for Subscriber's investigation)
  • Driver performance score per vehicle (0–100 composite)
  • Performance tier classification: Elite / High Performer / Good Driver / Needs Improvement
  • Bonus incentive modelling and net ROI on driver incentive programme

On-Demand Vehicle Efficiency Report

Pool Included

Request a focused efficiency report for any vehicle for any date range for which data is available. Ideal for investigating anomalies or reviewing a specific vehicle's history.

  • Specified vehicle registration and fleet details
  • Custom date range as requested
  • Actual km/L achieved vs vehicle-specific benchmark
  • Total kilometres driven and litres consumed for the period
  • Estimated fuel waste and financial impact at wholesale & retail price
  • Comparison against fleet average for the same period (where data is available)

On-Demand Speed Violation Report

Pool Included

A targeted report on driver behaviour for any specific vehicle and time period. Useful for disciplinary processes, insurance matters, or driver improvement tracking.

  • Total speed violation events for the specified period
  • Maximum speed recorded and over-speed duration (where available from telematics)
  • Estimated financial impact of violations
  • Driver risk rating based on violation frequency and severity
  • Comparison against the vehicle's own historical pattern (where prior data is available)
How the On-Demand Pool works: Your monthly pool equals the number of active vehicles in your fleet. Pool reports may be used for any combination of efficiency or speed reports. Unused pool reports expire at month-end. Reports beyond the pool are billed at R 150 per report at month-end.

Conditions of Service

Terms & Conditions

A full Master Service Agreement is provided before services commence and must be signed before onboarding begins. The key terms are summarised here for reference.

Billing & Payment

  • Monthly retainer is billed in advance on the 25th of each month.
  • The first invoice includes the once-off setup fee plus Month 1's retainer.
  • Payment is due within 7 days of invoice date.
  • All amounts exclude VAT. Dynamic Fleet Reporting is not currently VAT registered.
  • On-demand report overages are billed at the end of the calendar month in which they were requested.

Service Delivery

  • Reports are delivered electronically via email within 24–48 hours of receipt of all required data inputs.
  • The Subscriber is responsible for submitting complete and accurate data timeously.
  • Reports are generated from data supplied by the client — accuracy depends on data quality.
  • No guarantee of specific monetary savings is made. Outcomes depend on the Subscriber acting on recommendations.

Anomaly Detection & Limitations

Where unusual or unexpected fuel consumption patterns are identified, Dynamic Fleet Reporting will flag these in the relevant report. The Consultant makes no determination as to the cause of any anomaly. Anomaly flags are informational only. It is the sole responsibility of the Subscriber to investigate, verify, and take appropriate action.

Cancellation & Notice

  • Either party may terminate the agreement with 30 days' written notice.
  • There is no long-term contract lock-in.
  • Should fleet size cross a tier threshold, the new rate applies to the full fleet from the following billing cycle, with 30 days' written notice.

Confidentiality

All fleet data, reports, and client information are treated as strictly confidential. Dynamic Fleet Reporting will not disclose or share any client data with third parties without the Subscriber's prior written consent, except where required by law.

Data Submission Obligations

  • The Subscriber is responsible for providing accurate odometer readings, fuel logs, and GPS/telematics data per reporting cycle.
  • Late or incomplete submissions will delay report delivery — the 24–48 hour window begins on receipt of complete data.
  • A dedicated client email channel is provided for all data submissions and communications.
Important: This information pack is a summary only and does not constitute a legal agreement. The full Master Service Agreement governs the relationship between Dynamic Fleet Reporting and its clients. A copy of the SLA will be provided before onboarding begins.
