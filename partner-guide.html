Welcome to DFR

Referral Partner Programme

Welcome to the Dynamic Fleet Reporting Referral Partner Programme. This guide covers everything you need to know — from accessing your portal and sharing your link, to understanding your commissions and earning free months for your own business.

Read through this once and you'll have a clear picture of how everything works. There are no complicated processes — your side of things is simple by design.

Your role in one sentence

You refer fleet operators and transport companies to DFR. We handle everything from there. You earn 15% of their monthly fee — every month, for as long as they stay.

Contact DFR

WhatsApp or call: 083 314 9592  ·  Email: dynamicfleetreports@gmail.com

Your Referral Link

The most important thing you have

Your referral link is your unique identifier. Every lead that comes through it is automatically tagged to you — you never need to tell DFR who you referred.

What it looks like

https://dynamicfleetreports-bit.github.io/DFR/discover.html?ref=ref_your_name

The part after ?ref= is your unique code. It's based on your name and never changes.

Where to find it

1
Open your Partner Portal
Go to your portal link (sent to you via WhatsApp when you were activated). Bookmark it on your phone — you'll use it often.
2
Your link is at the top
The green box at the top of your portal shows your full referral link. Tap 📋 Copy Link to copy it instantly.
3
Paste it anywhere
WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn, email, SMS — anywhere. When someone opens it and fills in the contact form, you're credited automatically.
Set it as your bio link

On Instagram and TikTok you can only have one link in your bio. Put your referral link there. Every follower who taps it lands on the DFR discovery page with your code already attached.

The Reel Builder

Build TikTok and Instagram Reels slides

The Reel Builder lets you create professional vertical slide graphics (9:16 format) for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels — all branded with DFR's identity. Your referral link is automatically appended to the caption when you copy it.

How to open it

In your Marketing Hub, click the 🎬 Reel Builder button next to your referral link. It opens in a new tab with your referral code already attached to the URL — so your link is pre-loaded.

Building your reel — step by step

1
Load a preset script or start blank
Choose from 3 pre-written scripts (The April Bomb, The Driver Nobody Talks About, The Maths) or start from scratch. Scripts pre-fill all your slides and caption automatically.
2
Pick a visual style
Choose from Dark & Bold, Aggressive (red), Clean Navy, Light & Clean, or Custom (pick your own colours). The preview updates live.
3
Edit your slides
Each slide has a headline and supporting text. Click a slide to preview it on the right. Use | in the headline to create line breaks. Add, delete, or reorder slides as needed.
4
Add a background (optional)
Search Unsplash for real photos (trucks, highways, fuel pumps) or pick one of the 10 fleet-themed SVG illustrations. Set opacity with the slider. Apply to one slide or all slides.
5
Preview your reel
Click ▶ Play Preview to watch your slides cycle through automatically. Adjust the speed (Slow / Normal / Fast / Very Fast) to see how it feels as a reel.
6
Download your slides
Download a single slide or all slides at once as PNG images (1080×1920px — full TikTok resolution). A loading screen shows progress when downloading all slides.
7
Copy your caption
Click 📋 Copy Caption + Hashtags. Your caption, hashtags, and referral link are copied together — paste it when uploading to TikTok, Reels, or wherever you post.

Posting your reel

TikTokUpload slides one-by-one as a Photo Slideshow, or combine them in CapCut (free app) to make a video reel
Instagram ReelsUse CapCut to combine slides into a video, then upload as a Reel
Facebook ReelsSame as Instagram — combine in CapCut then upload
CapCut is free

CapCut is a free video editing app available on Android and iOS. Import your downloaded slides, set each one to 1–2 seconds, add music if you like, and export. It takes less than 5 minutes.

Your link auto-appends to captions

When you click Copy Caption, your referral link is always included at the bottom. You never need to add it manually.

How to Refer Someone

Three ways a referral gets to DFR

There are three ways to refer someone. Any of them work — use whichever fits the situation.

Method 1 — Share your link (recommended)

Send your referral link to anyone you think could benefit from DFR. When they open it, they land on the DFR discovery page with a banner saying they were referred by you. When they fill in the contact form, your code is submitted automatically.

https://dynamicfleetreports-bit.github.io/DFR/discover.html?ref=ref_your_name
Best for

Social media posts, WhatsApp messages, email, bio links. The person just opens the link and fills in their details — nothing extra needed.

Method 2 — Tell them to mention your name

If someone contacts DFR directly (WhatsApp, phone, email), they just need to say "I was referred by [your name]" and DFR will link them to you manually.

Best for

In-person conversations, phone referrals, situations where sharing a link isn't practical.

Method 3 — Brief DFR directly

Pass on DFR's contact details and WhatsApp DFR first so they know to expect the call and who sent them.

Only activated clients count toward your commission and annual bonus. A prospect who enquires but never signs up does not earn you anything. Follow up with DFR if someone you referred has gone quiet.

How Your Commission Works

15% of every invoice — for life

You earn 15% of the monthly retainer for every client you refer — every month, for as long as they remain a DFR client. There is no time limit and no cap.

How the retainer is calculated

TierFleet SizeRate per VehicleExample
Tier 11 – 15 vehiclesR200 / vehicle / month10 vehicles = R3,500/month
Tier 216 – 30 vehiclesR175 / vehicle / month20 vehicles = R5,000/month
Tier 331+ vehiclesR150 / vehicle / month40 vehicles = R7,500/month

What you earn

Your rate15% of monthly retainer (excl. setup fees)
When it's dueAfter the client's invoice is confirmed paid
How it's paidEFT to your nominated bank account
When it's paidWithin 7 business days of the monthly commission run
How long it runsEvery month the client remains active — indefinitely
Real example

You refer a 20-vehicle transport company. Monthly retainer: R5,000. Your commission: R750/month · R9,000/year. Refer 5 similar clients: R3,750/month · R45,000/year.

What commission is NOT paid on

  • The once-off R2,500 setup fee — excluded from commission calculation
  • Any month where the client's invoice was not paid
  • Clients who are still in prospect or pending status

Commission Statements

Monthly notifications and payment records

Every month when DFR runs commissions, you receive a WhatsApp notification with your statement. You don't need to chase — it comes to you.

Your monthly WhatsApp notification includes

  • Which month the commission covers
  • How many of your clients paid their invoice that month
  • Total MRR your referred clients generated
  • Your commission amount for the month
  • Confirmation that payment is coming within 7 business days

Viewing your full history in the portal

Your portal's Earnings Summary tab shows every month's commission — paid and outstanding.

Outstanding balance

If a month shows as Outstanding it means the EFT hasn't been processed yet. If it's been more than 7 business days since your notification, WhatsApp DFR on 083 314 9592.

Banking details

Commission is paid to the bank account you provided on your application. If your banking details ever change, WhatsApp DFR immediately before the next payment run.

Annual Performance Bonus

Reward for growing your portfolio

On top of your monthly commissions, DFR rewards partners who grow their referred client portfolio over the year. The bonus is assessed every November.

Bonus tiers

Portfolio Growth (Jan – Nov)Bonus
0% or declineNo bonus
1% – 10% growth1 additional month's commission
11% – 20% growth2 additional months' commission
21%+3 additional months' commission
Bonus example

5 clients at start of year → 7 at year end = 40% growth = 3 months bonus. Monthly commission from 7 clients = R5,250. Bonus = R5,250 × 3 = R15,750.

Tracking your bonus in the portal

  • Clients at start of year vs now
  • Current growth percentage
  • Which bonus tier you've reached (shown in gold)
  • Estimated rand value of your bonus
The tracker updates live

Every time a client you referred is activated, your growth percentage updates. Keep referring throughout the year — every activation pushes your percentage higher.

Your Partnership Agreement

Key terms in plain language

You signed a Referral Partner Agreement when you activated. Here are the key points in plain language.

What you agree to do

  • Promote DFR's fleet reporting services to transport companies and fleet operators
  • Direct potential clients to DFR using your referral link or DFR's contact details
  • Represent DFR's services accurately — only what DFR has told you it does

What you agree NOT to do

  • No pricing commitments — don't promise a specific price. Send them to DFR for pricing.
  • No managing clients — once they've signed up, it's DFR's relationship to manage
  • No competing — don't refer clients to similar competing services during the agreement or for 12 months after it ends
  • No impersonation — you're a partner, not an employee or director of DFR

Confidentiality

All client information, DFR's pricing, systems, and processes are confidential. Don't share client names, contact details, or any internal DFR information with anyone outside DFR.

If you want to end the agreement

30 days' written notice to DFR. Commission on existing clients continues for 3 months after termination.

Tax note

You are not an employee of DFR. You are responsible for your own tax on commission income. No UIF or PAYE is deducted from your payments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

I shared my link but don't see the prospect in my portal

Prospects appear once DFR has imported their contact form submission — typically within 24–48 hours. If it's been longer, WhatsApp DFR.

Someone I referred contacted DFR directly without using my link

No problem — WhatsApp DFR on 083 314 9592 with the person's name and company. If DFR can confirm it was your referral, they'll link it to you manually.

Can I refer someone I already know personally?

Yes, absolutely. Whether it's a business contact or a stranger who saw your post — all referrals are treated the same.

What if a client I referred cancels?

Commission stops when the client cancels. Your annual bonus counter is not reduced — only current active clients at November 30th count.

Can I refer multiple people?

Yes — no limit. More referrals = more monthly commission = higher chance of hitting the bonus tiers.

When does my commission get paid?

DFR runs commissions monthly. You receive a WhatsApp notification with your statement, and payment follows within 7 business days.

My bank details changed

WhatsApp DFR on 083 314 9592 immediately with your updated banking details before the next commission run.

Can I access everything on my phone?

Yes — the Marketing Hub, Reel Builder, and Partner Portal are all fully mobile-friendly.

The Reel Builder downloaded my slides but they look blurry on TikTok

The slides download at 1080×1920px which is full TikTok resolution. If they look blurry, make sure you're uploading the original PNG files and not compressed versions from your camera roll.

I can't access my portal link anymore

WhatsApp DFR and they'll resend your portal and referral links via WhatsApp.

Still have a question?

WhatsApp DFR on 083 314 9592 — we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
