How It Works
1
You refer a fleet operator to DFR. Share our number (083 314 9592) or simply tell them to mention your name when they make contact.
2
They sign up and become an active client. Once they’re active and their first invoice is paid, your referral counts.
3
We calculate your reward based on how many referrals you made that month and each referred client’s fleet size.
4
Your credit is applied automatically to your next invoice. If you earned a 50% discount, that’s applied too. If you hit a 10-referral milestone, a free month is added to your bank.
5
You receive a WhatsApp notification every time a reward is applied to your account, so you always know exactly where you stand.
Programme Rules
  • Only activated clients count — a prospect who never signs up does not earn you a reward.
  • Credits are calculated based on the first month’s retainer of each referred client, excluding setup fees.
  • The monthly tier multiplier applies to the number of referrals that activate in the same calendar month.
  • Free months are applied one per invoice cycle — you cannot stack two free months onto the same invoice.
  • Free months never expire and can be used any time, including saving for December or November.
  • All rewards are applied automatically — no claiming or paperwork required.
  • DFR reserves the right to amend the programme terms with 30 days’ notice to members.
