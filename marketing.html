💬 Direct Outreach — Start Conversations, Not Broadcasts

These scripts are for one-on-one messages only — not for posting publicly. The goal of every message here is simple: get a reply. Nothing more.

No links. No feature lists. No pricing. Just a short, human message that opens a door. The sales conversation happens after someone responds — not in the message itself.

The rule: Read each script out loud before sending. If it sounds like a marketing email, rewrite it in your own words first. It should sound like a real person talking.
